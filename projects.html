Our Latest Projects

  • The Happy Caterpiller

    Happy Caterpillar was our exhibit for the STEAM competition and STEM Family Day. The game is a recreation of the game Snake running on an Arduino microcontroller and displayed on a strip of NeoPixel LEDs. The cabinet was designed and painted in-house. To create the defined blocky look, a custom grid was 3D-printed and laid on top of the LED strips. For our efforts, we were voted as the favorite exhibit of the show and helped invigorate many people's enthusiasm for STEM.
